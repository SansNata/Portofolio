About Me

From the Stage to the Screen

Hello! I'm Ikhsan Hadi Winata. My journey is a unique blend of communication, business, and technology. I'm fluently bilingual in Indonesian and English, a skill I honed as the Vice President of my English Club. I have extensive experience commanding the stage as a Master of Ceremonies (MC) and public speaker for various events.

My entrepreneurial spirit sparked in high school, where I ran small but successful ventures reselling goods and even creating and selling helpful 'cheat sheets', teaching me valuable lessons in sales and business. This business experience drive, combined with my passion for technology, led me to front-end development. Across numerous organizations and events, I've developed strong leadership and teamwork abilities, thriving in collaborative environments where I can both lead and code.
